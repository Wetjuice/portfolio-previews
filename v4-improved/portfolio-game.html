What I Built

Key challenge: Deep customization systems that feel powerful to experts without overwhelming new players. The solution was layered disclosure: surface the highest-impact options first, reveal depth progressively.

The Problem & Result

Problem: Post geo-beta data showed D3 player retention below targets. The primary progression loop was either too slow, too confusing, or both.

Process: Led a cross-disciplinary pod of 10 developers — analyzed player behavior data, identified friction points in the core loop, and redesigned reward pacing and milestone clarity.

Result: +12% D3 player retention after redesign shipped. A meaningful lift in a metric that directly impacts LTV on a mobile game.
