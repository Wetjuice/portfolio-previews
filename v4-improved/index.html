Design Thinking

What I Believe

▶
Ideas Are Abundant. Execution Is Scarce.
"Design isn't about generating good ideas — everyone has good ideas. Great design is identifying which ones to pursue, and knowing how to make them real. The gap between concept and shipped feature is where the actual work lives."
✦
Emergence Is the Medium's Superpower.
"The most memorable gaming moments were never scripted. They happened because the systems were good enough to generate something nobody planned. No other medium produces that. That is the target."
◑
A Game for Everyone Is a Game for No One.
"Every decision to broaden appeal dilutes conviction. Define your audience with precision. Design for that person with full commitment — then go make that game."
⬡
Systems Punch Above Their Weight
"The best cost-to-experience ratio isn't bigger budgets — it's systems that create more than they cost."
↺
Validate Before You Polish
"Fidelity is a reward for ideas that survive contact with reality. Find the fun first."
◎
Leadership Is Selection, Not Invention
"Build teams smart enough to generate options you wouldn't have thought of yourself. Then make the call."
